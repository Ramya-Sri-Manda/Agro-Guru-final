Agricultural Infrastructure Funds

The role of infrastructure is crucial for agriculture development and for taking the production dynamics to the next level.

Agri Infrastructure Schme

  • 🌱 Development of such infrastructure shall also address the vagaries of nature.

  • 💰 The Honrable Finance Minister announced on 15.05.2020, 1 lakh crore Agri Infrastructure Fund for farm-gate infrastructure for farmers.

  • 🚜 Financing facility of Rs. 1,00,000 crore will be provided for funding Agriculture Infrastructure Projects at farm-gate.

  • 🏗️ Impetus for development of farm gate & aggregation point, affordable.

  • 🛑 There are various Exclusion Categories for the scheme.

SCHEME EXCLUSION

  • 🌾The Scheme will be operational from 2020-21 to 2032-33.

  • 🌾Loan disbursement under the scheme will complete in six years.

  • 🌾The fee for this coverage will be paid by the Government.

  • 🌾All loans under this financing facility will have interest subvention of 3% per annum.

  • 🌾This subvention will be available for a maximum period of 7 years.

  • 🌾In case of loans beyond ₹ 2 crore, then interest subvention will be limited up to ₹ 2 crore.
