Our Agricultural Courses

Explore the fascinating world of agriculture with our comprehensive course.

Precision Agriculture

Use of GPS technology, sensors, and data analytics for precise farm management. The goal is to enhance sustainability by making more informed decisions based on real-time data.

Climate-Smart Agriculture

Strategies to adapt to and mitigate the impacts of climate change. Sustainable and resilient farming practices.It involves the integration of climate change adaptation and mitigation strategies into the planning and implementation of agricultural systems.

Vertical Farming and Controlled Environment Agriculture

Growing crops in controlled environments like greenhouses and vertical farms. Hydroponics, aeroponics, and aquaponics

Organic and Sustainable farming

Sustainable farming practices that minimize environmental impact. Organic certification and agroecological principles.

Enterpreneurship in Agriculture

Business planning and financing for agricultural startups. Implementing variable rate technology for optimized resource use.Innovation and entrepreneurship in the agricultural sector.

Remote Sensing in Agriculture

Remote sensing is used to forecast the expected crop production and yield over a given area and determine how much of the crop will be harvested under specific conditions

Crop Science

Crop science is the study of the world's major food, feed, turf, and fiber crops and their environment. It is a broad discipline encompassing breeding, genetics, production, and management.

Integrated Pest management

Integrated pest management (IPM) is a science-based process that uses a variety of methods to control pests. The goal of IPM is to reduce the negative effects of pest control on people, property, and the environment.
